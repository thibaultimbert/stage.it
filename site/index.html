Five steps
  1. 01

    Install in one click

    Add the Stage.it plugin to Codex.

  2. 02

    Provide your store URL

    Enter your Shopify store URL so Codex can connect to your store. Authentication is done by you, using your browser. All secure.

  3. 03

    Let Codex guide you

    Codex walks through connection, product selection, and photo generation with you.

  4. 04

    Native image generation

    Stage.it relies on native image capabilities using the gpt-image-2 image model. No dependency required. A paid ChatGPT subscription is recommended for high volume of image support.

  5. 05

    Review and upload

    Review the generated photos, approve your favorites, and they are uploaded to your store for you.
